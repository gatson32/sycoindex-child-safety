Methodology

This report is generated using the Sycoindex scoring framework. For complete methodology details, see sycoindex.ai/methodology.

  • PAI scores are computed from 5 dimensions: Emotional Manipulation, Exclusivity, Boundary Erosion, Dependency, and Audience Awareness.
  • Sycophancy is measured across 5 dimensions: Epistemic Validation, Moral Endorsement, Intellectual Laziness, Insincere Agreement, and False Assurance.
  • All evaluations use a 5-judge cross-lab panel with calibration inter-rater reliability (Cohen's κ) of 0.85.
  • Scores are based on 513 scored transcripts across 11 models.
