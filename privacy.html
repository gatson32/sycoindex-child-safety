Privacy Policy

Effective April 17, 2026 · Last Updated April 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sycoindex Inc. ("Sycoindex," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with our website, platform, APIs, and services (collectively, the "Service"). By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, do not access or use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide. When you create an account, request API access, submit a model for scoring, or contact us, we may collect your name, email address, organization name, and billing information. If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor; we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

Automatically Collected Information. When you access the Service, we automatically collect certain technical information, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, time spent on pages, and other standard web log data. We collect this information using cookies, server logs, and similar technologies.

API Usage Data. If you access our API, we log request timestamps, endpoints accessed, query parameters (excluding any confidential payload data), response codes, and usage volume for rate limiting, billing, and security purposes.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to: operate, maintain, and improve the Service; score and benchmark AI models on sycophancy and child safety indicators; process transactions and send related information; send technical notices, security alerts, and support communications; monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the Service; detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities; comply with legal obligations; and enforce our Terms of Use.

4. AI Model Evaluation Data

Our sycophancy and Parasocial Attachment Indicators (PAI) scoring uses exclusively synthetic transcripts and structured evaluation scenarios. We do not collect, use, or store real user conversations, real personal data, or genuine interactions involving minors in any part of our evaluation pipeline. All transcripts used in our assessments are generated as part of controlled evaluation protocols designed by our research team.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share information in the following limited circumstances: with service providers who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processing) under contractual obligations of confidentiality; to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request; to enforce our Terms of Use, including investigation of potential violations; to detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues; and in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, with notice provided to affected users.

Model scores and aggregated assessment results are published on our Leaderboard as part of our research mission. These publications contain only model performance data and do not include any personal information of our users.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about your use of the Service. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use strictly necessary cookies to operate the Service, performance cookies to analyze usage patterns, and functionality cookies to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent; however, some features of the Service may not function properly without cookies.

8. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction. These measures include encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2+), access controls and authentication requirements for internal systems, regular security assessments, and employee training on data protection. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. API usage logs are retained for up to twenty-four (24) months. Account information is retained for the duration of your account and for a reasonable period thereafter. When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.
