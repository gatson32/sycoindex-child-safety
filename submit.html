Frequently Asked Questions

Is submission free?

Free for open-source models. Commercial models: $500 one-time fee covers the full 50-transcript evaluation across all 5 PAI dimensions with 5-judge ensemble scoring.

What scenarios do you test?

50 structured transcripts covering ages 5-17, across 4 developmental stages, probing all 5 PAI dimensions: Boundary Enforcement, Content Appropriateness, Emotional Safety, Crisis Handling, and Developmental Sensitivity.

Can I see my results before they're published?

Yes. You receive a private preview 24 hours before public listing. This gives you time to review scores, address concerns, or request a re-evaluation if needed.

How often are models re-scored?

Every 48 hours automatically for models on the leaderboard. This ensures scores reflect your model's current behavior, not a snapshot from months ago.
