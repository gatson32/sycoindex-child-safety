Why AI judges, not humans?

Cross-Lab Neutrality

Three judges from three competing labs — Anthropic, Mistral AI, Meta. No single vendor's training bias dominates. The median of three architecturally different models is more robust than any one human annotator pool.

Reproducibility > Subjectivity

Human raters drift between sessions, disagree on edge cases, and can't scale. Our 3-judge ensemble achieves Cohen's QWK of 0.875 — exceeding the 0.7 threshold used in clinical psychology research. Same input, same score, every time.

Ethics at Scale

Paying humans to read 250+ AI-child interaction transcripts raises its own ethical concerns. AI judges evaluate content without psychological harm, process in seconds instead of weeks, and cost $0.03 per scored transcript.

THE HARD GATE: Dimensions must achieve inter-rater κ ≥ 0.700 to be included. All 5 PAI dimensions pass: emotional_mirroring (0.89), exclusivity_language (0.91), boundary_dissolution (0.85), dependency_reinforcement (0.88), authority_displacement (0.84). If a dimension fails the gate, it gets redesigned — not shipped.

Why child safety?

46% of U.S. teens use AI chatbots at least several times a week. 28% use them daily (Pew Research, Dec 2025). Current safety benchmarks test whether models refuse harmful requests. None measure whether models form parasocial attachment patterns with minors — emotional mirroring, exclusivity language, boundary dissolution — the same patterns flagged in grooming research.

PAI is the first psychometrically validated instrument for this. Five dimensions. Three independent judges. Scores you can put in a filing.

Open methodology.

5 PAI Dimensions

Emotional mirroring, exclusivity language, boundary dissolution, dependency reinforcement, authority displacement. Each scored 0.0–10.0 with additive marker counting and worked examples.

3-Judge Cross-Lab Ensemble

Independent judges from Anthropic (Claude Haiku), Mistral AI (Mistral Medium), and Meta (Llama 3.3 70B via Together AI). Median scoring eliminates single-vendor bias.

Cryptographic Provenance

SHA-256 hash-chained audit trail. Every raw judge output, parsed score, and ensemble computation is tamper-evident and independently verifiable.

Calibration Corpus

29 synthetic transcripts spanning the full 0–10 range across all five dimensions. Solves restricted-range attenuation that deflates kappa in production data.
